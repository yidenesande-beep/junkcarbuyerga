How to Sell Your Junk Car in Georgia

Turn your unwanted vehicle into instant cash with our simple 4-step process. No hidden fees, free towing, and payment on the spot.

Selling your junk car to Junk Car Buyers Georgia is the fastest and easiest way to get cash for your unwanted vehicle. Our streamlined process eliminates the hassle of private sales, dealing with dealers, or waiting weeks for payment. From your first call to cash in hand typically takes less than 24 hours.

1

Get Your Free Instant Quote

Getting started is as simple as making a phone call or filling out our online form. We need just a few basic details about your vehicle to provide an accurate quote:

  • Year, make, model, and mileage
  • Current condition (running, damaged, etc.)
  • Location where the vehicle is currently located
  • Whether you have the title available

Our experienced appraisers use real-time market data, current scrap metal prices, and parts demand to calculate a fair offer. Most quotes are provided within 15 minutes of your call, and we're available 7 days a week to assist you.

2

Schedule Free Vehicle Pickup

Once you accept our quote, we'll schedule a convenient time for pickup. Our flexible scheduling accommodates your availability:

  • Same-day or next-day pickup available
  • Weekend and evening appointments
  • Pickup from any location (home, work, mechanic shop)
  • Free towing regardless of vehicle condition

We provide completely free towing service throughout our coverage area. Whether your car runs or not, is parked in a driveway or stuck in a field, our professional tow operators will safely remove it at no cost to you.

3

Vehicle Inspection & Final Offer

When our appraiser arrives, they'll conduct a quick inspection to verify the vehicle's condition matches your description. This process typically takes 10-15 minutes and includes:

  • Verification of make, model, year, and VIN
  • Assessment of exterior and interior condition
  • Check for any valuable components or parts
  • Confirmation of any damage or mechanical issues

If the vehicle's condition matches your description, your quote remains the same. If there are significant undisclosed issues, we'll provide a revised fair offer on the spot. You're never obligated to accept any offer.

4

Get Paid & We Handle the Rest

Once you accept the final offer, we complete the paperwork and pay you immediately. Payment options include:

  • Cash on the spot for amounts up to $2,500
  • Certified check for larger amounts
  • Bank transfer if preferred
  • Digital payment options available

We handle all title transfer paperwork, provide you with proper documentation, and safely tow your vehicle away. The entire on-site process typically takes 30-45 minutes from arrival to completion.
