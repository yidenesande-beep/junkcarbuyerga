Our Story: From Family Business to Georgia's Premier Car Buyers

Founded in 2009, Junk Car Buyers Georgia began as a small family operation with a simple mission: provide honest, fair, and fast cash offers for unwanted vehicles throughout the Atlanta metropolitan area. What started as a weekend side business has grown into Georgia's most trusted junk car buying company, serving thousands of satisfied customers across 50+ locations.

Our founder, Mike Rodriguez, started the company after witnessing too many people getting ripped off by unscrupulous scrap dealers and low-ball offers from inexperienced buyers. With a background in automotive repair and a passion for customer service, Mike set out to create a company that would treat every customer with respect while offering truly competitive prices.
